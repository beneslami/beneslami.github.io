Research

My research interests center around:
1) Networked Systems design and implementation: I am interested in buidling routers and switches from scratch. Designing and building high speed networked devices which walks on the edge of the technology is my dream

2) Operating System: Being in the kernel level is a place where I am quite comfortable. Becasue I am able to see what is going on under( hardware level) and above (application level) me. 

3)Virtualization: Dynamic sharing and dynamic allocation of available resources is a topic which I can get a more understanding about what is God. Virtualization is a way of thinking and living. 

3) Distributed Systems: There is no centralized God. God is a distributed concept. You can understand and feel the god when you deeply understand what distributed system is.  

4) Datacenter Networking: Datacenter is the intersection of networking, Operating Systems, Virtualization and Distributed Systems.
